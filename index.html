about us

Trusted Industrial Solutions Since 2012

Since 2012, CB Globle India has been a trusted name in industrial management and innovative solutions, specializing in the plastics sector—particularly blow molding, injection molding, and extrusion operations. With over 25 years of combined industry expertise, our technical and managerial teams deliver exceptional consultancy, technical guidance, and project management services tailored to your unique industrial needs.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive industrial solutions from machinery procurement to installation, commissioning, and operational optimization for plastics manufacturing.

Machinery & Procurement

Procurement and supply of specialized plastic machinery, molds, and utilities. We collaborate with top manufacturers across India, Taiwan, Germany, and China to deliver cost-effective solutions tailored to your requirements.

Erection & Commissioning

Professional erection, installation, and commissioning of machines with comprehensive technical support. Our expert teams ensure seamless integration and optimal performance of your manufacturing units.

Training & Consulting

Implementation and training on ISO standards, GMP, and LEAN manufacturing principles. Technical consulting and Annual Maintenance Contracts for blow, injection, and extrusion molding operations.
