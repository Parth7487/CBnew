About Our Consultancy

At CB Globle India, our team brings over 25 years of specialized experience in blow molding technology, serving top manufacturers across packaging, water tanks, and customized plastic solutions. We are committed to guiding clients at every stage, from initial concept to successful project execution, by delivering tailored, scalable, and commercially viable solutions.

Why Choose CB Globle India?

  • Proven expertise in packaging solutions from 50ml to 1000L
  • In-depth knowledge of water tank and custom product manufacturing
  • Advanced technical capabilities including double cavity and specialty molding
  • Strong track record in project management and techno-commercial consulting
  • Solutions designed for performance, efficiency, and profitability

Our Commitment

We emphasize a complete partnership approach—from initial design and machinery procurement to installation, commissioning, and ongoing operational optimization—ensuring your manufacturing units run efficiently and produce high-quality outputs.

With our dedication to delivering competitive pricing without compromising on product excellence, we ensure maximum customer satisfaction and long-term business growth.
