About CB Globle India

Pioneering Industrial Solutions Since 2012

Our Journey & Mission

Since 2012, CB Globle India has been a trusted name in industrial management and innovative solutions, specializing in the plastics sector—particularly blow molding, injection molding, and extrusion operations. With over 25 years of combined industry expertise, our technical and managerial teams are equipped to deliver exceptional consultancy, technical guidance, and project management services tailored to your unique industrial needs.

Our founder's background in mechanical engineering and extensive professional experience with leading companies in water tanks and pipes manufacturing fuels our commitment to quality and innovation. At CB Globle India, we emphasize a complete partnership approach—from initial design and machinery procurement to installation, commissioning, and ongoing operational optimization—ensuring your manufacturing units run efficiently and produce high-quality outputs.

We proudly collaborate with top machine and mold manufacturers across India, Taiwan, Germany, and China, tailoring solutions that best fit client requirements and industry standards. Our dedication is rooted in delivering competitive pricing without compromising on product excellence, ensuring maximum customer satisfaction.
