Material Options

HDPE - High-Density Polyethylene

Ideal for rigid containers, bottles, and tanks. Excellent chemical resistance and durability for industrial and food applications.

LDPE - Low-Density Polyethylene

Perfect for flexible applications, films, and bags. Good impact resistance and flexibility for various end uses.

PP - Polypropylene

High-temperature resistant material suitable for industrial applications, automotive components, and food packaging.

PET - Polyethylene Terephthalate

Food-grade material commonly used for beverage bottles and packagings with excellent barrier properties.
