V Type Granulator - Features & Specifications

Design & Cutting Performance

  • Unique scissors type structure, conductive to improve cutting efficiency
  • Blades are made of high-strength alloy tool steel with high hardness and excellent abrasion resistance
  • Blades engineered for long durability and extended service life

Noise & Thermal Control

  • Feeding hopper with back and front double layered insulation for effective noise handling
  • >30HP default equipped with chamber cooling system
  • Cooling system effectively reduces heat in chamber and shaft
  • Prevents material melting and fusion for superior product quality
  • Ensures long durability of equipment components

Motor & Bearing Options

  • >100HP models equipped with high-quality self-aligning bearings
  • Bearings feature excellent lifetime running characteristics
  • >75HP models with cylinder system on feeding hopper and mesh screen
  • Cylinder system available as optional upgrade for other models

Safety & Additional Features

  • Equipped with multiple safety protection devices to ensure safe operation
  • Optional auto collecting system for better material recovery
  • Optional dust separating system for environmental compliance

Applications

Suitable for all kinds of large-scale hollow products including leather, PE film, PET bottles, and various plastic materials. Ideal for high-volume plastic waste processing and industrial granulation with precision engineering for continuous operation.
